About Us

Who Are We?

Viman Enterprises is a certified license holder company which provides all types of weighbridge maintenance services for Truck Scale or Road Weighbridges, Coil Weighbridges and Laboratory Weighing Scales. Our strong network of well-trained and experienced engineers is spread across Mumbai and it's Suburbs.

Our Services include Annual Weighbridge Maintenance contracts with Installation, On-Site Modification, Calibration, Painting, Stamping, and Troubleshooting. We also attend Emergency Call based complaints for weighbridges.

Our Mission

We at Viman Enterprises believe in harnessing passion, responsibility and seriousness to contribute towards a better environment and healthy society for present, and future.

Our Vision

Viman Enterprises was formed in 1990 with a mission to provide fast, effective and cost friendly services for businesses to enhance efficiency and quality.

Our Values

  • To us, our employees are like our family. So we offer an unprecedented solutions in balancing work and life.
  • We value our clients and always strive to deliver desired results and help them achieve their goals.
  • We value honesty, integrity, hard work and dedication in offering our services.

We are just 4 hours away to help you with your needs. Our team is professionally trained, licensed, and insured to handle your problems.
